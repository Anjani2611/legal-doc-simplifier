SERVICE AGREEMENT

Whereas, Party A wishes to engage Party B to provide legal document simplification services; and Whereas, Party B agrees to provide such services under the terms and conditions outlined herein;

Now, therefore, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

1. SERVICES: Party B shall simplify legal documents provided by Party A into plain language that is understandable to the general public.

2. CONFIDENTIALITY: Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information exchanged during the term of this Agreement and for a period of two years after termination.

3. PAYMENT: Party A agrees to pay Party B according to the fee schedule provided separately.
